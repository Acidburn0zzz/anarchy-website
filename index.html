About Anarchy

Anarchy Linux came up with one goal: to simplify the installation of Arch Linux. Initially called "Arch Anywhere", it was adding followers who forgot to sweat when they wanted to install Arch Linux on their machines. Currently the project has been reborn with the hope of becoming more than just an installer.
